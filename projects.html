Hospital Operation Management System

Major Project - NITK Surathkal, 2021

Internet of Things
Embedded Coding
Web Development
Data Analytics
Digital Signal Processing
Deep Learning

We developed an IoT-based 'Smart Hospital' environment by integrating a Django-based web server with an embedded system capable of recording patient vitals like temperature, SpO2, heart-rate & ECG. The website assisted doctors & nurses in realtime & continuous patient health monitoring, electronic health records & hospital analytics. We could further identify abnormalities in heartbeat patterns using a trained multi-layered LSTM.

Collaborator: Rajamani Ayyer.
