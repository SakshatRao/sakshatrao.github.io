More About Me

Music

I have around 15 years of experience playing the keyboard. I enjoy playing songs by ear, for I not only get to enjoy how the song sounds but also how technically brilliant it is. I have experience playing other musical instruments like Guitar, Saxophone, Harmonica & Harmonium, but I still have a lot to learn and practice.

One of my desires (like many other instrumentalists) is to be a self-sufficient musician - that is, be able to play/record songs all by myself. I realize I need to be very good with different instruments for that, but hey, I have all my life to work towards that!

I have attempted covering a couple of my favourite songs which I share on my YouTube channel for multi-instrumental covers. Feel free to take a look at some of my favourite covers.

Poetry

I enjoy writing poems because of how one gets to creatively manage words. While writing a poem, you need to choose the right words, strictly follow the rhyming scheme, maintain the theme & structure of the poem - while simultaneously trying to tell a story through your words. Writing the perfect poem is often tough, but that is what I enjoy.

Although I have had some of my poems appear in school & college magazines, I have admittedly not been able to write poems as consistently as I wished to, but I try to pursue this skill as much as I can. Hopefully, I can be inspired enough to write poems regularly.

Sports

Since early childhood, I have been active in sports. I might not be the best at sports, but I want to be good and fit enough to continue playing in the future.

Living up in Mumbai, I have played my fair share of gully cricket. I love playing football & badminton as well. I have represented my school in football at the Mumbai district level while participated in several school-level 800m races. I recently started participating in mini-marathons. Although I was huffing & puffing at the end, hopefully one day, I can get to complete a full marathon.
